# Tsamaea (Go)

**Tsamaea** is a localized cab and bus booking system designed to revolutionize public transport in under developed countries

The platform aims to empower independent drivers by organizing them into professional service structures, eliminating government bottlenecks, and creating a reliable travel ecosystem.

---

## üéØ Our Aim
* **Job Creation**: Providing a platform for drivers to find consistent work.
* **Urban Decongestion**: Decreasing the reliance on private vehicles through efficient public options.
* **Professionalization**: Moving from stand-alone drivers to organized, reliable transport organizations.
* **Reliability & Security**: Ensuring passengers have access to safe and dependable transport.
